Boiler And Central Heating Breakdown:

UK Gas-Serve Limited is a trusted and professional organization offering UK households an essential safety and peace of mind central heating boiler check service. Helping our clients by ensuring that their boiler is working safely, efficiently and reducing the risk of costly repair bills in the future as well as ensuring that your boiler is working to its maximum efficiency to help manage ever increasing gas prices.

Not having your boiler checked often lead to faults, higher energy bills and ultimately costly boiler replacement, the average cost of a replacement central heating boiler for a three bedroomed house can be as much as £3,750 and even a 25% reduction in boiler efficiency can add and average of £270 per year.

What does our Certified Boiler Check entail?

At a time, convenient to you in the 7 working days following your purchase one of our Safe Gas Register approved engineers will visit your home and carry out a full boiler inspection including:

  • Inspection of the entire structure of your chimney flue. Poor ventilation is the primary cause of buildup of dangerous Carbon Monoxide levels that can be potentially lethal to you, your family or pets.
  • We’ll check your boiler is igniting properly. Poor Ignition leads to costly reduction in efficiency and risks your safety.
  • We’ll test your boiler – including your heating and hot water. Ensuring everything is functioning properly to maximize Energy Efficiency.
  • We’ll assess the controls to make sure they’re working, as they should be. For proper operation, preventing maintenance and unnecessary Energy Use.
  • We’ll check how energy efficient your boiler is. And report on any possible improvements to Increase Efficiency and Reduce Energy Costs
  • Check and report on any harmful gas emissions. For your complete peace of mind and safety.
