HISTORY

FOUNDED IN 2001

Jordan Pioneers Multimedia Production Company (JP) is a content-based production company located in Amman, Jordan. We seek to create media which inspires critical and creative thought, initiating beneficial changes in the global community. This ambition has led us to produce projects as varied as children’s television, drama, documentaries, social awareness campaigns, promotional spots and public service announcements (PSA’s).

With over 15 years of experience in providing first-rate services to the production of many international projects, Slate has become a leading rental house for professional film equipment, supplying its customers with full equipment and crew for feature films, commercials, music videos, and television productions.

Ever since its inception, Slate has contributed to the production of many international feature films such as “Transformers 2” (by Michael Bay), the Oscar-winning film “The Hurt Locker” and “Zero Dark Thirty” (by Katheryn Bigelow), “Rosewater” (by Jon Stewart) and “Redacted” (by Brian de Palma), as well as “Star Trek: Discovery”, “Rogue One: A Star Wars Story” and “All the Money in the World”.

Since 2018, we are the proud content creators and producers of Ahlan Simsim, the Jordanian version of Sesame Street. Ahlan Simsim, the International Emmy Award Nominee of 2021 in the Kids is a children's show targeting children ages 3-8 and their caregivers throughout the Pan-Arab region, with special focus on the needs of the displaced and host communities in Iraq, Jordan, Lebanon, and Syria.

The show features brand-new, Arabic-speaking fun and friendly characters with storylines that both displaced and host community children in the region can relate to.
